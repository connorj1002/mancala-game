INSTRUCTIONS

  • Players alternate taking turns
  • On player's turn, select a cup of stones on player's side
  • One stone will be placed in each subsequent hollow, counter-clockwise around the board, until stones from that hand run out
  • Stones may be placed in the player bank, but should always skip the opponent bank
  • If the last stone from hand lands in player's bank, player takes another turn
  • If the last stone from hand lands in an empty cup on player's side, player collects stones from adjacent cup on opponent's side and banks the stones
  • Once a player has cleared all of their cups, the game ends
  • That player should collect and bank all remaining stones from opposing cups
  • The player with the most stones in their bank wins the game

TIPS

  • This game plays best on full screen size
  • When selecting a cup to play, click on the space within the cup - NOT on a stone
